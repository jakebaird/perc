Granite Tiles — Bringing Nature into your Home

Granite tiles have become increasingly popular for use both in the home and commercial properties. That’s partly because they are made from an entirely natural material and so make people feel closer to nature.

This type of tile does of course also have other features and benefits that have caused the material to become so popular. The tiles bring a certain luxury to any room, can be a stunning addition to all decors and are entirely durable and practical.

The Benefits of Granite Tiles for Brisbane Properties

Granite is formed from igneous rock that resulted from volcanic reaction followed by a cooling process under great pressure. As a result, it’s an extremely hard-wearing stone that’s resistant to staining, scratching and chemicals. This makes it the perfect material for tiles in any location, whether in the kitchen, bathroom or living areas and for floors as well as walls. It can be walked on, worked on and is resistant to high temperatures.

Granite is naturally formed and contains quartz, feldspar and mica as well as traces of other minerals. Because of this, it’s available in a wide range of colours, from black through to numerous lighter shades, and has sparkling elements due to the presence of bronze and copper. This gives it a luxury appearance that can add glamour to any room.

Granite tiles fit in wonderfully with modern and traditional décor, and the shimmering quality of the stone can create a stunning feature wall or floor. The tiles usually have a high gloss surface and are made from the best quality granite, ensuring they’ll look great in whatever location you choose to have them.

Unlike some stone products that are porous and need to be sealed, granite has a completely non-absorbent surface. It’s therefore ideal for use in the kitchen, which is why it’s one of the most popular materials for benchtops, and you can complement them with matching or contrasting floor or wall tiles. The durability results from its formation around 300 million years ago and demonstrates the tiles will last forever.

As well as their exceptional appearance, granite tiles are easy to clean and maintain. Simply wash down periodically with warm water and soap or a non-abrasive cleaning fluid and your tiles will continue to look like new.

Getting the Best from your Granite Tiles

To truly benefit from the many features of granite, you need to have your tiles installed by a professional company that knows what it’s doing. At PERC Tiling, we have many years’ experience of fitting granite tiles throughout Brisbane and understand the challenges they present.

Because they are a natural product, every tile is unique and will have differences in appearance. Whilst this is a definite advantage because it means every tiled wall or floor is truly different, it is necessary to ensure those differences are subtle so that the tiles blend with each other when in place. Be assured that we will ensure the tiles are sufficiently similar that they provide an acceptable mixture of colour and shade.

No matter how high the quality of product, it won’t look great if it’s not installed properly. All our tilers are fully conversant with granite and tiles generally and will give you a finish you’ll be truly proud of.
