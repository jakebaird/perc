Floor Tiling Repair

Any kind of floor or wall tiling requires maintenance and repair from time to time. At PERC Tiling, we not only sell and install a wide range of tiling solutions, we carry out all kinds of repairs on floor, saving you the necessity of re-doing your tiling from scratch.

Each client’s floor tile repairs are evaluated on a case-by-case basis by our highly skilled and competent repair team, who carry out repairs to flooring failures that occur such as water or fire damage, incorrect installation, or general flooring wear and tear. 

We carry out the following repairs on solid wood floors and engineered hardwood floors:

  • Removal of scratches, dents and gouges on wood surfaces can be buffed out and refinished
  • Repair of loose floorboards
  • Repair of subfloor squeaks
  • Repair of a tripping hazard
  • Refinish and re-surface
  • Incorporation of additional space
  • Incorporation of additional design.

Whatever your repair need is, let our team handle it for you. We are careful to take into consideration the environment in which the repairs require to be carried out, making sure that as little disturbance as possible is imposed on your home or establishment during the period of the repairs.

We carry out both scheduled and urgent unscheduled repairs. If you wish to find out more about our services and to get in touch with us, we can be reached on email at info@percgroup.com.au., or call us on 1300 414 162.
