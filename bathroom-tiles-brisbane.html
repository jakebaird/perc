Bathroom Tiles for all Tastes

A bathroom has varying uses for different people. For some, it’s a place for a quick wash or shower while for others it’s a place for relaxation, somewhere for a restful soak and a bit of pampering.

To reflect those differing views, there is a wide range of tiles that enable people to create the bathroom they desire. Both wall and floor tiles come in a range of sizes, styles, colours and finishes that can complement or add contrast for a stunning appearance.

Bathroom Tiles in Brisbane — the Choice is Yours

Bathrooms are naturally damp places due to a combination of steam from the hot water and bath water being splashed about. Tiles are therefore a great choice for the walls and floors because the water can easily be wiped off and prevent mould growth.

Bathroom tiles originally tended to be white and plain, which had the advantage of showing dirt that needed to be cleaned and so helping with good hygiene. Although there are many more options now, white is still a great choice because it creates a crisp and clean look that can be made visually stunning with coloured grouting.

Ranges are now much more extensive, with colours ranging from vibrant to subdued and patterned tiles for special effect. Choose contemporary greys for a spa feeling, neutral colours for a timeless look or bold colours and patterns to make a statement about your personality.

Although colours are a personal choice, remember that others will also use the bathroom and you want to make the right impression. Also be aware that the tiles will last for an awful long time and you don’t want to be changing them simply because you grow to hate the colour choice or it has gone out of fashion. Choose carefully, therefore, for an effect that will last.

The trend lately has been for tiles to get bigger and this has practical as well as aesthetic advantages. Large tiles tend to be great for small bathrooms because they make them look bigger. That’s due to them having fewer grout lines, and they’re also easier to clean than lots of small tiles.

How Bathroom Tilers in Brisbane Can Help Your Choice

At PERC Tiling, we’ve had lots of experience with bathroom tiling and can help you achieve the best and most practical outcome. That extends to installing non-slip floor tiles that can prevent the slips that occur all too often in the damp atmosphere of a bathroom. Natural stone, in particular, helps prevent slips due to its textured surface.

Using professional and experienced bathroom tilers such as ourselves will ensure your bathroom is as beautiful and practical as you want it to be. In fact, with the advice, we can give you and the knowledge we can apply, we often create bathrooms that far exceed the expectations of their owners.

Your bathroom will be warm and welcoming, cool and contemporary or whatever you want it to be. It will be a practical place or somewhere you can relax in peace but will also be easy to maintain and safe to use as well as somewhere you can impress your guests. Put your faith in us, and we’ll create a bathroom you can be truly proud of.
