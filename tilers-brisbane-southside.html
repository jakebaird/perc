Waterproofing, is it Necessary?

A common question that we’re asked about tiling is whether or not waterproofing tiles is necessary, and the answer varies.

It depends on the job but for the most part pool tiles must always be waterproofed, it is highly preferable to waterproof bathroom tiles (both floor and wall), and everywhere else is optional.

Waterproofing is a great solution to prevent dampness from getting under your tiles and causing damage, so in our eyes, it’s always worth considering.
