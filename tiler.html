We are looking for Tilers to join our growing team.

PERC Tiling is a highly regarded wall and floor tiling company that specialises in high end tiling. We are a solid, dynamic and progressively growing team of highly skilled tilers and support staff. Our team works closely together supporting and sharing knowledge. PERC Tiling has a strong culture around honesty, responsibility, good communication and having fun. We pride ourselves in delivering quality outcomes.

Key Selection Criteria:

  • Wall and floor tiling licence, safety white card and drivers licence
  • Suitable transport and own tools as needed to fulfil your role
  • Minimum 10 years experience in all aspects of tiling including stone, mosaic, porcelain, etc.
  • Excellent written & verbal communication skills, including a professional phone manner
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Can-do, positive attitude
  • Knowledge in all aspects of tiling, waterproofing & joint seal details

Responsibilities
  • Communicate with client regarding job details and finishes
  • Oversee job runnings and materials management
  • Lay, grout, screed, waterproof, silicone and other associated aspects of wall and floor tiling
  • Overseeing quality control of projects
  • Communicate to team leaders via phone and email regarding job details
  • Personal time sheet management
Experience

10 years experience is required.

Employment Terms

Full time, in Brisbane.
