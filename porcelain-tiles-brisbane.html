Porcelain is Surprisingly Durable

If you are looking for a porcelain tile expert, then you’ve come to the right place. We are fast creating a name in Australia as a leading provider of superior quality tile services. We provide ceramic, porcelain, marble, stone and wood tiling for various residential and commercial purposes.

People are often unaware of the difference between porcelain and ceramic tiles. Porcelain tiles are ceramic tiles but are much harder and shinier than the normal ceramic tiles.

The difference between porcelain tiles and ceramic tiles is that they are made from clay that is denser, finer, and has been heated to much higher temperatures than ceramic tiles. The result is that this deceptively fragile-looking tile is actually much harder than granite, and far more durable.

As porcelain is denser, it is far less prone to scratches, in addition to being more durable than ceramic tiles. This makes it an ideal choice for counter-tops, shower and bath areas and wall coverings.

Porcelain is also a very attractive material and can be dyed in different colours, from soft pastels to deep hues. It can be painted on or made to resemble stone making it excellent material for floor tiling, and at a fraction of the cost.

We at PERC Tiling will be happy to give you an estimate for installing any kind of porcelain tiles you may require. Our workmanship is second to none, and our commitment to quality ensures that you receive high-quality work finishes.   

Maintaining Porcelain Tiles

One of the greatest positives to porcelain is that during the dying process the paint seeps deep into the material which means that even over time it maintains its colour and brilliance. Furthermore, porcelain is also extremely stain resistant and resistant to moisture.

This makes cleaning porcelain tiles relatively easy. Any stains and spills simply require a wipe with a damp cloth to completely remove traces of any accidental mess, and often a mild detergent mixed with water is sufficient to keep it sparkling. This make porcelain perfect for surfaces that experience a lot of wear and tear or for high traffic areas.

While porcelain tiles maintain their pristine appearance and durability for a long time there still remains some chance of chipping or breaking. However, these tiles are reasonably simple to repair. You should also ensure that grout is sealed completely to reduce the chance of mould taking hold and always remember to clean the grout when you clean the tiles.
