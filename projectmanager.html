Estimator / Project Manger

  1. Home

We are looking for an estimator/ project manager to lead our growing team.

PERC tiling is a highly regarded wall and floor tiling company that specialises in high end tiling. We are a solid, dynamic and progressively growing team of highly skilled tilers and support staff. Our team works closely together supporting and sharing knowledge. PERC Tiling has a strong culture around honesty, responsibility, good communication and having fun. We pride ourselves in delivering quality outcomes.

Key Selection Criteria:

  • Bachelor or Diploma in Building & Construction highly regarded
  • Experience in Project Management / Contracts Administration
  • Excellent written & verbal communication skills, including a professional phone manner
  • Excellent attention to detail
  • Strong negotiating skills & dispute resolution
  • Strong time management & organisational skills
  • Ability to develop & maintain strong relationships
  • Highly computer literate
  • Can-do, positive attitude
  • Relevant work experience in construction industry
  • Knowledge of relevant building codes & terminology
  • Knowledge in all aspects of tiling, waterproofing & joint seal details

Responsibilities
  • Preparation of quotations and tender submissions for a wide range of different projects and dollar values.
  • Take off quantities
  • Letting of Orders
  • Contract Administration
  • Project Management
  • Prepare schedules & works programs
  • Liaising with clients, contractors and suppliers
  • Asset Management & Financial Reporting
Experience

Ideally 2 — 10 years experience is required.

Employment Terms

Full time, in Brisbane.
