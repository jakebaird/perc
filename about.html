About Us


Peter Russ
Founder & General Manager

Peter Russ, founder and managing director of PERC Tiling has 18 years of experience in tiling. He has a reputation for exceptional quality and a high standard of workmanship. The demand for his expertise has seen him expand to a team of over ten employees.

Peter has handpicked a team of highly skilled industry professionals that each have a gift in their craft that contributes to the synergy of team PERC. Aside from his passion and drive to build Brisbane’s leading luxury stone tiling company, Peter enjoys surfing, fishing, the occasional beer and is married with two young children.

About Us

Since its establishment in 2013 PERC Tiling has grown steadily and now has a team of highly skilled and professional tilers boasting a variety of experience in natural stone, travertine, marble, granite, porcelain and mosaics. 

PERC works with a variety of award winning builders and commercial developers adding to its wealth of knowledge and experience that is applied to every tiling project. We strive to always have open communication and invest time and energy to understand our clients vision and needs.

Community

PERC Tiling supports World Vison’s work in Emergency relief and Livelihoods. 

Syria Refugee Crisis
Image Copyright World Vision

Natural Disasters and Conflict have a devastating effect on children and communities in developing countries.


PERC Tiling has supported World Vision’s emergency response to situations like the Syria Refugee Crisis and the Nepal Earthquake.

Apart from emergency situations, PERC Tiling also supports World Vision’s work in Livelihoods. Livelihoods help create economic opportunities and improve access to nutritious food, so families living in poverty can transform their lives and break the cycle.
