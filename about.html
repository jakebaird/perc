About Us

Peter Russ
Founder & General Manager

Peter Russ, founder and managing director of PERC Tiling has 18 years of experience in tiling. He has a reputation for exceptional quality and a high standard of workmanship. The demand for his expertise has seen him expand to a team of over 10 staff.

Peter has handpicked a team of highly skilled industry professionals that each have a gift in their craft that contributes to the synergy of team PERC. Aside from his passion and drive to build Brisbane’s leading luxury stone tiling company, Peter enjoys surfing, fishing, the occasional beer and is married with 2 young children.

About Us

Since its establishment in 2013 PERC tiling has grown steadily and now has a team of highly skilled and professional tilers boasting a variety of experience in natural stone, travertine, marble, granite, porcelain and mosaics. 

PERC has worked with a variety of award winning builders and commercial developers adding to its wealth of knowledge and experience that is applied to every subsequent project. We strive to always have open communication and invest time and energy to understand our clients vision and needs.
